Teacher’s Bridge Pro 🍎

​The AI-powered curriculum partner designed for high-compute, data-driven lesson planning.
​Teacher’s Bridge Pro bridges the gap between your long-term curriculum goals and your daily lesson plans.
By leveraging Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Claude 3.7 Sonnet, this platform ensures your classroom instruction is always aligned with your pacing guides, IEP requirements, and behavioral goals.

​🚀 Key Features
​📅 Date-Aware Planning: Intelligent calendar integration that aligns daily lessons with your specific yearly pacing guides.
​📚 Intelligent Document Ingestion: Upload PDF/DOCX syllabi or use the Snap Curriculum feature to OCR physical worksheets and notes directly into your knowledge base.
​🧠 Pedagogical Engine: Standardized generation output including Essential Questions, CFU protocols, 3-tier scaffolding, and Behavioral Pivots.
​🛡️ Secure SaaS Architecture: JWT-based authentication ensures your curriculum data remains private and persistent.
​🎨 Academic Minimalism: A distraction-free, mobile-responsive UI designed for teachers on the go.

​🛠 Tech Stack
​Inference Engine: Claude 3.7 Sonnet
​Database & Auth: Supabase / PostgreSQL
​Infrastructure: Kilo.ai Full-Stack SaaS Deployment
​Frontend: Responsive UI with Academic Minimalism design system

​📋 How It Works
​- Register: Create your secure account.
​- Upload: Drag and drop your monthly/yearly pacing guides into your Curriculum Library.
​- Capture: Use your mobile camera to scan physical resources.
​- Generate: Select a date, input your topic, and let the AI build a lesson perfectly aligned with your curriculum goals.
